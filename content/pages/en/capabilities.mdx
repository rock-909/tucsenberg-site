## A Website and Lead Foundation for a Small B2B Company

The demo shows the pieces a company needs before a public launch: pages, offer story, inquiry path, deployment proof, and owner traffic visibility.

## Credible Public Pages

Home, capabilities, process, about, contact, privacy, and terms pages start from a working structure.

## Clear Offer Story

Product, service, resource, and custom-project examples show how real offers can be explained.

## Lead Capture Path

Contact and inquiry flows stay visible, testable, and ready for real destinations.

## Cloudflare Deploy

The supported route is Cloudflare Workers through OpenNext.

## Owner Reporting Visibility

Once credentials are configured, the owner can confirm real Cloudflare traffic is visible.

## Replacement Guardrails

Docs and checks keep sample identity, proof, legal copy, and deployment values from being treated as real launch truth.
