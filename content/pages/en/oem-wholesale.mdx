Most flood protection importers juggle three or four Chinese factories — one for aluminum, one for plastics, one for bags and seals. We run a coordinated factory pool so you don't have to: five product lines, one contact, one QC standard, one mixed container.

## How the factory pool works

We are not a trading company reselling whatever's available, and not a single factory pushing one material. The product designs and specifications are ours; production runs in a fixed set of specialised partner factories — extrusion, injection moulding, PVC welding, technical sewing — each doing the process it's actually built for, under one spec system:

- **One spec sheet per product**, whichever factory produces it. Your reorder next year matches your order this year.
- **One QC standard.** Pre-shipment inspection across all lines, one report to your inbox before anything sails.
- **Mixed-container consolidation.** Gates + boxwall + bag cartons + spares in one 40'HQ — the freight math that makes smaller trade orders work.
- **Spare-parts continuity.** Seals, joints, fins, end-caps stay available across years — your installed base stays serviceable, your customers stay yours.

## What private label actually includes

Not just a sticker. Depending on the line:

- **On-product**: moulded-in logo (ABS), printed shells and sleeves (bags, tubes), plank marking (aluminum)
- **Packaging**: your carton artwork, pallet presentation, inserts and manuals in your layout
- **Documentation**: spec sheets and QC reports issued under your brand, ready to hand to your customers
- **Boundaries, stated plainly**: custom-branded and custom-cut items are final sale; moulded logos carry a one-time mould contribution, quoted before you commit.

## How to verify us

We're a Chinese supplier asking for your trust, so here's how to check instead of hoping ([who we are](/about)):

1. **Factory audits welcome** — in person or live video walk-through of any factory in the pool.
2. **References on request** — we'll connect you with buyers who've shipped with us.
3. **Paid samples before any volume** — judge the product, not the website.
4. **Third-party inspection accepted** — appoint any agency of your choice for pre-shipment inspection; we schedule around them, not against them.
5. **Your spec on the paperwork.** Model, material, dimensions and packing written into the quotation and invoice — so what you approved is what's enforceable, not what a listing photo implied.
6. **Production evidence before it sails**: photos and video of your actual order at packing, plus the QC report — the sample you approved is the bulk you get.
7. **Start small — trial orders welcome.** Our minimums are cartons and pallets, not containers; qualify us with a trial order before you commit real volume. Scale when the product earns it.
8. **A written warranty** - 3 years on materials and workmanship for standard durable product lines; consumables and custom items follow the product-specific terms in the [Warranty Policy](/warranty).
