## Service providers \{#service-providers\}

The final site owner must confirm the providers used for hosting, analytics, email delivery, form handling, spam protection, CRM, and any file upload or storage workflow.

Any cookies, analytics, tracking, or consent banner behavior must be documented before public launch.

## Data retention \{#retention\}

Retention periods are not final in this placeholder. The site owner must define how long inquiry records, form submissions, email records, analytics data, and uploaded files are kept.

Expired records should be deleted or anonymized according to the final policy and applicable law.

## International transfers \{#international-transfers\}

## Security \{#security\}
