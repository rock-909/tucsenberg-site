Verify us

We publish what can be checked, and nothing that can't.

Every spec table on this site carries numbers you can hold us to — wall thickness, unit weights, tested UV exposure (ASTM G154). Your spec is written into the quotation and invoice, so it is enforceable, not decorative.

Contracts, invoices and bank details always carry the same legal name: Jiangsu Tucson Borg Technology Co., Ltd. If any document shows a different name, stop and contact us.

Third-party inspection is welcome — SGS, TÜV, QIMA — and so are factory audits, in person or by video. Judge us by the sample and the spec sheet, not the country line on the box.

FAQ · 9 entries, 3 shown

Questions buyers actually ask

What is your minimum order?

It depends on the line: boxwall from one carton, flood bags from 300 bags (six cartons), tube dams from a single section; gates are quoted per opening schedule. We don't ship single retail units — freight economics punish everyone. Smaller need? Ask about joining our next consolidated shipment.

You're made in China — how do I know the quality is real?

Geography doesn't decide quality; spec discipline does. Ours is checkable: published numbers on every product page, your spec written into the quotation and invoice, third-party inspection welcome, and a paid sample — credited against your first order — before any volume.

Can we audit the factories?

Yes — factory audits are welcome, in person or by video. References available on request.
