Security Disclosure Policy for Example Showcase Company

How to report:
- Send reports to security@example.com
- Include reproduction steps, impact, and any proof-of-concept material

What to expect:
- We aim to acknowledge receipt within 5 business days
- We will validate the report and follow up if more detail is needed
- We will coordinate on disclosure timing for confirmed issues

Good-faith research:
- Please avoid privacy violations, destructive testing, or service disruption
- Please do not publicly disclose an issue before we have had a reasonable chance to investigate and remediate it
